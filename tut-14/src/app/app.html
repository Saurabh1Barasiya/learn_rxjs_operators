takeUntil

It will emit the value until inner obevable emit the value.

Inner observable ne kuch bhi bola ya complete hua → takeUntil ne main stream ko maar diya!

Jese hi inner observable (jo takeUntil ke andar diya hai) emit karega – chahe kuch bhi emit kare (number, string, object, void, error, ya complete) – waise hi main stream turant mar jaayega!
